Requirements

Who Can Apply?

Resident Status

Students must reside within the designated district boundaries. Priority is given to local residents.

Age Requirements

For Kindergarten, students must be 5 years old by September 1st of the enrolling year.

Transfer Students

Must provide previous academic records and a behavior report from their past institution.

The Process

How To Apply

1

Online Registration

Create a parent account on our portal. This will be your hub for all application materials and tracking.

2

Document Submission

Upload birth certificates, proof of residence, immunization records, and previous report cards.

3

Campus Tour & Interview

Schedule a mandatory tour where we can meet your child and discuss their goals and our program.

4

Acceptance & Enrollment

If accepted through our lottery or direct process, you will receive an offer letter to complete the enrollment.
