North Campus Academy

Downtown District Grades K-8 STEM Focused

North Campus Academy is a premier institution dedicated to fostering innovation and critical thinking. Located in the heart of the city, we provide a rigorous STEM-based curriculum designed to prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Academic Focus

Empowering Future Innovators

Our curriculum goes beyond standard academics. We integrate hands-on project-based learning with advanced technology to create an engaging environment.

  • Comprehensive coding and robotics classes starting in 1st Grade.
  • Advanced math tracks for accelerated learners.
  • Environmental science integrated with local community projects.
  • Strong emphasis on literacy and communication skills.
